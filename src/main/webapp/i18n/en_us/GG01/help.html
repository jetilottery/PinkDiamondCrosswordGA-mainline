Pink Diamond Crossword

Diamonds are forever as you try to match letters in this glittering game. Stop the wheels spinning to reveal letters and see how many words you can create. Extra letters can be awarded if the Pink Diamond lands alongside your letters!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of Pink Diamond Crossword is to complete as many words as possible on the crossword grid.

Pressing the stop button in the centre of the 4 spinning discs causes them to stop. This reveals 3 letters which are shown in the Your Letters display. If a pink diamond lands in alignment with the 3 letters then a single letter is revealed in the Bonus Letters display.

The revealed letters can now be selected on the crossword grid. If HINTS is toggled on then highlights will appear around the revealed letters on the grid. If AUTO MARK is toggled on then the letters on the revealed letters will be automatically selected.

If 3 words or more are completed on the crossword grid then a prize is awarded to the player. This is shown in the Prize Table.

The game ends after 6 turns revealing at least 18 letters.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
